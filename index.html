Our Story

A love rooted
in simple moments

What started as a friendship in church became the most beautiful chapter of their lives. Through almost nine years of knowing each other — shared laughter, quiet evenings, and countless moments together — Chester and Elbee's love simply blossomed, in its own time, in the most beautiful way.

Now, after nearly a decade of growing together, they are ready to begin their greatest journey yet. They invite their family and closest friends to celebrate the day they say "I do."
